eggs

ingredients

This a pretty simple recipe. I eat this for breakfast nearly everyday. The ingredients consist of the following.

  • 6 Eggs
  • 15g of olive oil
  • Some cheese in my fridge

The steps to making the recipe are as follows.

  1. Crack eggs into a pan.
  2. Add in olive oil.
  3. Add cheese.
  4. Stir until ready.
  5. Serve. Add some seasoning on top if you feel spicy.
